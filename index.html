About Me

I'm a Roblox builder with a passion for creating immersive, functional, and visually stunning environments. Whether it's a neon-soaked city, a medieval fortress, or a horror-themed laboratory, I transform ideas into playable experiences.

My Projects

Graveyard

This map is a low-poly graveyard environment featuring stylized gravestones, dry twisted trees, cartoonish rocks, and a clean UI panel on the side. It's designed with a playful yet eerie atmosphere in mind — ideal for simulator, pet-collecting, or tycoon-style games with Halloween or spooky themes. The vibrant lighting and fog-free environment make it readable and performant even on lower-end devices.

Abandoned City

This environment portrays a city in ruins, surrounded by tall protective walls and reclaimed by nature. Ideal for wave defense or zombie survival modes, it includes smoke effects, destroyed buildings, and military ruins. Great for games focused on post-disaster or apocalyptic scenarios where players must strategize and defend.

Futuristic City

This cyber-themed city map features glowing neon architecture, layered highways, and a dark ambient atmosphere. Perfect for tower defense or sci-fi combat games, it includes modular skyscrapers, dynamic lighting, and a clean futuristic UI layout for wave-based gameplay. Designed to run efficiently while maintaining a strong visual identity with bold lighting accents.
